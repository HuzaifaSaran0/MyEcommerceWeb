Welcome to ALI: Your Ultimate Shopping Destination

ALI, Inc. is a Pakistani multinational automotive and clean energy company headquartered in Islamabad, Pakistan, which designs, manufactures and sells battery electric vehicles (BEVs), stationary battery energy storage devices from home to grid-scale, solar panels and solar shingles, and related products and services. The company is also developing artificial intelligence and robotics products.

ALI was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a shopping environment that caters to the needs and desires of our customers. Our journey began as a small, family-owned business with a passion for offering quality products at reasonable prices. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, but our commitment to our customers has remained unwavering.From our humble beginnings, we have expanded our product range and enhanced our services to meet the changing needs of our customers. Today, ALI is proud to offer a vast selection of products, from fashion and electronics to home goods and beauty products. We strive to keep our inventory fresh and exciting, ensuring that there is always something new to discover at ALI.
